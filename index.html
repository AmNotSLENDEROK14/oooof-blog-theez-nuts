Places I recommend you to visit (singapore)

Marina Bay

i had visited it on Dec 2021

Marina Bay is a bay located in the Central Area of Singapore, surrounded by the perimeter of four other planning areas, the Downtown Core, Marina East, Marina South and Straits View. The area surrounding the bay itself, also called Marina Bay, is a 360 hectare extension to the adjacent Central Business District.

Review On The Movies

Avengers Endgame
excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it today with my kids and they loved it. You should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will miss 40% of the fun. You should watch Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panther, Age of Ultron, Ant-Man and the Wasp before watching Avengers: Endgame. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel superhero movies. You will enjoy it a lot. There is, at the beginning of the movie, a (spoiler alert) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe. The movie is lengthy, and it does run for 3 hours all worthwhile


DEMON SLAYER
excellent film about tanjiro and other guys to fight demons

As a demon slayer, Tanjiro and Nezuko embark on several dangerous missions in order to keep the world safe and ultimately defeat the Demon King Muzan who created all demons and murdered their family. Demon Slayer is an incredible anime that's revered by many fans as the best shonen series in anime history. Much like Attack on Titan, the reason why Demon Slayer is popular is because of how human the themes and the cast are. They all have flaws, they can't all do everything, but they do all experience loss, hope, and are seen persevering till the very end.
